Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a support ticket?

Log in to your account, go to Tickets → Create Ticket, fill in the required information including service type, and submit. Your ticket will be tracked in real-time.

How do I purchase a service?

Go to Services, click "Buy Service" on any service card, fill in the dummy payment form, and click "Complete Purchase". Your service is activated immediately.

What is Cloud Storage and how do I use it?

Cloud Storage provides 500GB of encrypted storage with collaboration features. Purchase it from Services, then create tickets for any storage-related support needs.

Can I upload a profile photo?

Yes! Go to Profile → Edit Profile to upload or change your profile photo. We support JPG, PNG, and GIF formats up to 5MB.

What services does ORBiS offer?

ORBiS offers IT Support, Customer Support Desk, Software Maintenance, Website Management, Cloud & Hosting Support, Technical Consultation, and Cloud Storage.

Do I need an account to access Help Center?

No! Help Center is accessible to everyone. You can browse our FAQ and submit inquiries without logging in.
